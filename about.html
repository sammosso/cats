    Intro

    Cats are social creatures and love to be pet and some don't. Did you know that there are over 45 breeds of cats? On this website page you will learn all about cats.

    Cats' Diet

    A pet cat will normaly eat dry cat food, and/or wet food. Outdoor cats might hunt there own food. Cats can eat meats, fish, and some other food like blueberries, eggs, and melons. The meats cats would eat include turkey, pheasent, beef, and chicken. Some of the fish cats eat are Tuna, Whitefish Salmon, Mackerel, Trout, and Herring.

    life span

    there are many different breeds of cats and they all have life spans. A cat can last about 2-16 years in the wild or less than 2 years. An indoor cat can last 13-17 years or less than 13 years dependig on how well it is taken care of.
