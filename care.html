    • How To Take Care Of Your Cat

    Supplies

    Before you choose your cat you are going to have to gather supplies including:

    • kitty liter
    • cat food
    • wet cat food
    • toys
    • kitty liter box
    • water bowl
    • food bowl
    • scratch post
    • comb/brush
    • bed with blanket
    • collar with ID tag

    How To Feed

    Some cats like to eat wet and dry cat food and some like one or the other. So you should try to give your cat some of both and see if they will eat all of it or some of it. The best amount to put in thier bowl would be 1/3 cup of dry food and a spoon full of wet food. If your cat is over weight try giving it less food. If your cat is very thin try giving it more food, or if you have another cat try seperating them while they eat.

    Exercise

    Each cat needs to exercise and the best way might be to let your cat go out side with a coller on. If you dont want your cat to go outside you can try playing with your cat.

    Kitty Liter Box

    No cat wants to use a dirty liter box, so it is up to you to clean it and change the liter. You should scoop it every day and change out the liter every two to three weeks.

    Vacations

    Every time you go on a long vacation, and dont bring your cat you can't leave it home alone. Your going to want a cat sitter to at least check on the cat. The cat sitter will have to play, feed and do the cat box while you are gone. Instead of having a random person sit your cat make sure you have a friend or company come ove to your house while your gone.
